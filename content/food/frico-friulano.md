## Ingredients

- 230g semi-hard mature cheese grated
- 230g potato grated
- 1 onion sliced
- Olive oil, 2 tbsp
- Salt and pepper

## Process

- Fry onion in oil, medium high heat.
- Add potato, salt and pepper.
- Cook for 10 minutes while mixing.
- Combine in cheese, set to medium heat.
- Smooth into a disk.
- Cook both sides until crisp, 5 mins each side (flip with plate)
- Cut like pizza, serve hot
