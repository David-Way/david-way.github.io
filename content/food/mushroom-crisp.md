## Ingredients

- mushroom (chanterelles, trumpet, oyster, or sliced button)
- oil
- spice mix (for example, garlic, smoked paprika, oregano, brown sugar)

- Preheat oven to 200Â°C
- Toss mushrooms in spice mix and oil
