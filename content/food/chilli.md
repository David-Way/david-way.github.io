## Ingredients

- onion, large
- pork or beef mince 400g
- garlic cloves 3-4
- cocoa powder 
- beef stock
- tomato paste
- canned tomatoes 
- canned beans, any two cans
- rice wine vinegar or similar 
- maple syrup
- cheese
- tortilla chips 

## Process

- Thinly chop and fry onion 
- Salt and brown meat add pet, then remove 
- Fry garlic and add seasoning 
- Add stock, optionally include maple syrup, reduce
- Add tomato paste, cocoa and vinegar
- Add beans and simmer
- Add meat and onions and canned tomatoes 
- Slow cook on high for 90mins or low for 4hrs
- Serve on any carb, with cheese and tortilla chips
