## Ingredients

- 200g dried lentils - washed, rinsed and drained
- 1 can plum tomatoes
- 1 Onion, diced
- 2 Shallots, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 500mls water
- olive oil
- spice mix - for example: chilli powder, onion powder, cumin, salt, pepper
- tortilla chips, crushed
- 200g cheese, grated (smoked version optional)
- tortilla wraps or soft taco shells
- 1 head lettuce, shredded
- greek yoghurt (optional)

### Sides

#### Salsa
- 2 tomatoes, diced.
- Â½ red onion, diced.
- Handful coriander, chopped
- 2 limes, juiced.

## Process

- Fry spices and oil in a pan
- Add onion shallots and garlic
- Add lentils, tomatoes and water
- boil and then simmer until lentils are soft
- Add tortilla chips and cool
- Add lentil mix and cheese to half a tortilla/wrap
- Fry each both sides in a pan
- Serve with salsa, shredded lettuce and yoghurt 

### Salsa

- Dice, juice and mix ingredients in a bowl
