Ingredients #

  • 700g beef mince
  • 50g Pancetta cube
  • 7 eggs
  • 2 onions
  • 2 tbsp Tomato paste
  • 200g cheddar cheese
  • 10 wraps
  • Spice mix (any) - for example: garlic, smoked paprika, onion powder, chilli powder, salt, pepper
  • Sauce (any) - for example: any premade jar sauce or homemade enchilada sauce (3 tbsp flower, olive oil, vegetable stock, cummin, garlic, can of tomatoes)

Process #

  • Spice the beef mince
  • Scramble the eggs, set aside
  • Grate cheese, set aside
  • Dice onion and fry in oil
  • Add pancetta, wait 30 secs, add tomato paste
  • Add beef, mix and brown
  • Add sauce and simmer
  • Build burritos
